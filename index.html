Brendon McCarron Single Barrel

This high proof, Bottled-in-Bond Whiskey was named for Brendon McCarron, the Irish immigrant who adapted his family's whiskey recipe to work with the grains he found in Kentucky. Brendon McCarron is one of the longest aged Bottled-in-Bond whiskeys available today, resting in the barrel through 40 Kentucky seasons. Critics agree that this is perfectly balanced Whiskey.

Proof: 100 Proof

Age: 10 Years Old
